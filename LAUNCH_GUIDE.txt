WELLNEST LAUNCH CHECKLIST

STEP 1 — Download and unzip the site
- Download the ZIP from ChatGPT.
- Right click and unzip it.
- The folder is called wellnest-site.

STEP 2 — Create a GitHub account
- Go to github.com
- Create a free account.
- Create a new repository called wellnest-site.
- Upload all files from the unzipped folder.

STEP 3 — Create a Vercel account
- Go to vercel.com
- Sign up using GitHub.
- Click Add New Project.
- Choose wellnest-site.
- Click Deploy.
- Vercel will create a free live URL.

STEP 4 — Buy a domain
Possible domain ideas:
- wellnestfinds.com
- shopwellnest.com
- wellnestliving.com
- lowtoxnest.com
- betterhomefinds.com

Buy one from:
- Namecheap
- GoDaddy
- Squarespace Domains

STEP 5 — Connect domain to Vercel
- In Vercel, open the Wellnest project.
- Go to Settings > Domains.
- Add your domain.
- Follow the DNS instructions exactly.
